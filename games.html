Check out these games

Dead Cells

Developer: Motion Twin

Genre: Action-platformer, Roguelike, Metroidvania

Overview: *Dead Cells* is a fast-paced action game combining roguelike and Metroidvania elements. Players explore procedurally generated levels, fight enemies, and collect powerful upgrades to progress further in the game. Its challenging combat and fluid mechanics have made it a standout in the indie gaming community.

Stardew Valley

Developer: ConcernedApe (Eric Barone)

Genre: Farming Simulator, RPG

Overview: In *Stardew Valley*, players inherit a dilapidated farm and work to restore it by growing crops, raising animals, fishing, and building relationships with town residents. Known for its charming pixel art, relaxing gameplay, and deep community interaction, *Stardew Valley* has become a beloved classic in the indie game scene.

Slime Rancher 2

Developer: Monomi Park

Genre: Farming Simulator, First-Person Shooter

Overview: *Slime Rancher 2* is the sequel to the beloved *Slime Rancher*, bringing even more slimes to collect and new areas to explore. Set in a new, colorful world called the Rainbow Island, players continue their adventure as Beatrix LeBeau, a slime rancher, capturing and raising new slimes. With expanded gameplay, including new features and environments, *Slime Rancher 2* refines the charm and fun of the original while introducing new mechanics to keep players engaged.

Hollow Knight

Developer: Team Cherry

Genre: Metroidvania, Action-Adventure

Overview: *Hollow Knight* is a Metroidvania action game that takes players through an interconnected world filled with enemies, secrets, and challenging platforming. The game is known for its atmospheric design, deep lore, and precise combat mechanics. *Hollow Knight* has become one of the most influential indie games in recent years.

Project Zomboid

Developer: The Indie Stone

Genre: Survival, Sandbox

Overview: *Project Zomboid* is a survival horror game set in a world overrun by zombies. Players must manage resources, craft tools, and survive as long as possible while fighting off zombies. The game’s deep survival mechanics and attention to detail have earned it a dedicated following over the years.
